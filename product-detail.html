And before you think about it , no this is not those cliche reviews praising this product. I'll just be stating my first impression. I liked the build. Looks quite oversized to be honest. But the sound quality is so crispy and clear. Active noise cancellation does its job but doesn't take care of really loud and abrupt noises. Comfort is amazing. Quite relatively light. Overall it's a five star product
Customer 2
Everything is just perfect about this headphone, but it's bit on the softer side, it could of been a bit louder and due to which the bass is also on a lower side but it's crystal clear sound. Loved the pair. Mic is just awesome for phonecalls, no disturbance and very clear on both ends. It's worth every rupee. I also have the JBL T460bt and that is an on ear headphone and the sound is mind-blowing, so if you want good bass and at a much cheaper price go for the T460bt, the sound is better than this and bass is powerful as well and much cheaper but this has a more crystal clear sound and good enough bass. Overall a very good pair, no regrets, just in love with this.
